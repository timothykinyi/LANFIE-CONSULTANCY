Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the needs of businesses and individuals in Kenya:

  • Tax Planning, Compliance, and Advisory

    We facilitate the preparation and filing of various taxes, including:

    • Value Added Tax (VAT)
    • Pay As You Earn (PAYE)
    • Income Tax
    • Capital Gains Tax
    • Rental Income Tax
    • Withholding Tax
    • Other statutory contributions (NHIF, NSSF, NITA, Housing Levy)

  • Bookkeeping

    Maintain accurate and up-to-date financial records for your business.

  • eTIMS / TIMS Compliance

    Ensure your business is compliant with the Electronic Tax Invoice Management System (eTIMS) and Tax Invoice Management System (TIMS) requirements.

  • Financial Audit

    Conduct thorough financial audits to assess the accuracy and integrity of your financial statements.

  • Payroll Management

    Manage your payroll efficiently, ensuring compliance with all statutory requirements.

  • Accounting Software & Systems Integration

    Implement and integrate accounting software solutions to streamline your financial processes.
