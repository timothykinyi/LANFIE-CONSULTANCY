About Us

Lanfie Consultancy is a consultancy firm domiciled in Kenya. We specialize in providing services with respect to bookkeeping, tax planning, and financial audit to organizations and individuals.

We are committed to delivering exceptional consulting services that enhance operational efficiency, foster sustainable success, and ensure regulatory compliance.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive tax and financial solutions that empower our clients to achieve their financial goals while maintaining compliance with the ever-changing tax laws in Kenya.

We strive to build long-term relationships based on trust, integrity, and excellence.

Our Vision

To be the leading tax consultancy firm in Kenya, recognized for our expertise, innovation, and commitment to client success.

We envision a future where businesses and individuals are empowered to thrive financially through strategic tax planning and compliance.
