Key Dates for Tax Compliance

20th of Every Month

Submission of VAT returns and payment.

9th of Every Month

Payment of PAYE for employees.

Last Day of Every Month

Submission and payment of withholding tax.

30th June

Annual tax returns for individuals and non-corporate entities.

31st December

Annual tax returns for corporate entities.

Quarterly Dates

Instalment tax payments are due on the 20th of the 4th, 6th, 9th, and 12th months of the year.
